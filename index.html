Smart Lighting System

SMART HOME

Smart lights

Smart lights are an integral component of modern smart homes, offering
advanced features that go beyond simple on/off functionality.

Remote control

Operate lights from anywhere using a smartphone or tablet. Turn lights on or off, adjust brightness, and change colors remotely.

Voice control

Use voice commands to control lights via virtual assistants like Amazon Alexa, Google Assistant, and Apple Siri. Set up voice-activated routines and commands for hands-free operation.

Color changing

Change the color of the lights to any shade on the spectrum. Use RGB and tunable white lights to match the mood or time of day.
